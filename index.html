What Motivates Us

Our Mission: The State of Veterans initiative advances research, public engagement, and academic collaboration focused on veterans, military service, and democratic society. Through scholarship, mentorship, and community partnerships, we seek to deepen understanding of how veterans shape and are shaped by American political and civic institutions while developing the next generation of veteran-centered scholars and leaders.

The Value We Add: We support veteran and non-veteran scholars who seek to better understand the political, social, and policy experiences of veterans, how military service shapes civic life, and how democratic institutions impact the lives of those who have served.

How We Transmit Value: We leverage our position in the Universities to design, conduct, and share research that advances knowledge about veterans, public policy, democratic institutions, and civic participation. Through research, mentorship, public engagement, and collaboration, we work to strengthen both scholarly understanding and public dialogue surrounding the state of veterans in America.

Our goals include:

  • The formation of a yearly, unified survey data set.
  • Connecting academic research institutions and veteran advocacy groups.
  • Conduct and publish research that gets to the heart of veteran matters.
  • Elevating veteran perspectives in research and public discussion.

Who We Serve

We welcome:

  • Veterans of all branches and eras
  • Active duty, National Guard, and Reserve
  • Student veterans (all branches and eras)
  • Universities, Research Institutions and veteran advocacy groups
  • Government agencies (i.e. Veteran Administration)
  • Anyone who works to support veterans

Programs & Initiatives

At The State of Veterans in Democracy Research Program we’re building a set of programs designed around connection, mentorship, and academic success.

SoV Academic Research

Advancing innovative research on veterans, democracy, public policy, and institutional trust while increasing interdisciplinary research productivity

Data Repository

Building a centralized national archive for veteran-focused datasets, surveys, policy research, and scholarly collaboration to support future research and public understanding.

SoV Advocacy

Bridging academic research with real-world impact through community partnerships, outreach initiatives, and philanthropic support that strengthen veteran services.

SoV Public Facing

Transforming research into public engagement through reports, media, storytelling, and educational programming that shape conversations about veterans.

Undergrad Research & Writing Symposium

Creating opportunities for undergraduate students to participate in research, strengthen academic writing, collaborate with faculty mentors, and present original scholarship through a dedicated symposium.

Writing Lab

Providing veterans and military-connected students with mentorship, writing support, and professional development while teaching the “invisible curriculum” of higher education.
